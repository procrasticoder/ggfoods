Company Profile

Established in the year 2006, at Udaipur, (Rajasthan, India) we, “G. G. Foods” have are reckoned as a prominent manufacturers and suppliers of a quality assured assortment of Vegetable Powder, Fruit Powder, Soya Products, Fruit Juice, Herbal Powder, Snack Foods, Vegetable Flakes, Amla Fruit Powder, Wheatgrass Powder, etc. Our range is processed using quality ingredients that are cleaned, dried and tested with the aid of advanced machines installed within our premises. These are then grounded and packaged in varying quantities. Our products are acclaimed for their adulterant free composition, rich flavor and supreme taste. Over the years, we have gathered a wide client base spread across the market. Our testing facility helps us in ensuring that quality products delivered at the clients' end. These products offered by us are known for their quality, freshness, refinement and cost effectiveness. To meet the diverse needs of the clients, we offer our products in different packaging options. Customization of goods is also available as per the clients’ need to provide them maximum satisfaction.

Quality Assurance

Maintaining the quality and reliability of the products is the first priority of our firm that makes us distinct from others and preferred choice of the clients. To gain the utmost client satisfaction, we sternly test our products on the several parameters like suitable temperature, proper ventilation and sanitation factors.
